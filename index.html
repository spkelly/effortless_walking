About

Gil was personally trained in The Feldenkrais Method® by Dr. Moshe Feldenkrais. He has been practicing private Feldenkrais Functional Integration® lessons and teaching Awareness Through Movement® classes since 1983. During that time, Gil has helped thousands of people gain relief from pain and learn how to move with more ease and coordination. He has taught in professional training programs around the world. He specializes in helping improve his client's tennis and hiking abilities.

He is currently offering both private sessions and group classes for clients in Boulder and the surrounding areas. Most importantly he loves to move and has been told he does it exceptionally well especially for a 69 year old.
